History of Kinder

Kinder is a brand of chocolate produced by Italian multinational confectionery company Ferrero. Created by Michele Ferrero, the Kinder name was originally developed in Alba, Italy in 1968. It was introduced first to the German and Italian markets that same year, before being sold in other countries in Europe.
